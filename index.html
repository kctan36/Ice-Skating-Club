What we do

We hang out! We formed this club to share the joy of skating. A place to find friends, learn tricks or just learn how to skate.

Who we are

My name is Steven Occupancy. The club started as with just a couple of friends that met each other through skating. As our number grew, we decided "Why not start a Ice skating club". And with that, here we are.

We made the club with the intention of befriending people with the same interest as us. So if you enjoy socialising and skating, what are you waiting for?
